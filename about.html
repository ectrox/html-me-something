About the Author

Early Life

As he notes in his new book, Woodson From The Streets: my life before rescue Woodson struggled finding his forever home. As a kitten, he spent a short time as a family cat until he snuck out and got lost. His world was about to turn upside down. After a month ot two of living off the land as a feral cat, Woodson was picked up by kind strangers and taken to the Humane Society. There, he spent almost his entire day in a small cage, only big enough for him and his litter box. He lived here for 8 months, watching cats all around him be adopted and go home with their new families'. This was a hard time for Woodson. Luckily, a girl named Emma came into the shelter looking to adopt the kitty who had been at the shelter the longest. Poor Woodson had been in the shelter longer than any of the other cats, but that meant he got to go home with Emma!!! The first night they lived together, Woodson joined her on the foot of her bed when it was time to sleep. Soon, he crawled up on her chest and purred while Emma pet him. From this moment, they both knew they had found their home together.
